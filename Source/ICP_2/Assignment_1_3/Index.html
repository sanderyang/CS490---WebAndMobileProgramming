Detailed Information on Top Ten Cats

Siberian Tiger

The siberian tiger weighs approximately 931 pounds (423 kg) and lives in Northeastern China, Russia, parts of Indian, and around the Himalayans.

Lion

The lion weighs approximately 598 pounds (272 kg) and lives in Sub-Saharan Africa with the exception of deserts and rainforests.

Jaguar

The jaguar weighs approximately 299 pounds (136 kg) and lives in Southern Arizona and New Mexico to northern Argentina and Northeastern Brazil.

Cougar

The cougar weighs approximately 264 pounds (120 kg) and lives in Southern Argentina and Chile to Southeastern Alaska.

Snow Leopard

The snow leopard weighs approximately 165 pounds (75 kg) and lives in Central Asia to Northwestern China and Tibet.
